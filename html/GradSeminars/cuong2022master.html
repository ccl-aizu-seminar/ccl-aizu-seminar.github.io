  • Title: LDPC-based HARQ Design for Optical Satellite-Assisted Internet of Vehicles

  • Presenter: NGUYEN Cuong

  • Abstract: The optical satellite-assisted Internet of Vehicles (IoVs) has recently attracted research efforts thanks to its wide range and high speed for sixth-generation (6G) wireless networks. However, one of the most critical issues in designing optical satellite communication systems is the uncertainty of atmospheric channels. This research plan presents a motivation for the design of hybrid automatic repeat requests (HARQ) in optical satellite-assisted IoVs requiring a simple but efficient error correction code (ECC). The design is based on low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes, a class of high-performance ECC with relatively low decoding complexity.
