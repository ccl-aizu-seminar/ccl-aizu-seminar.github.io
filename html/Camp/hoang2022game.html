  • Title: An Introduction to Network Games

  • Presenter: Dr. LE Hoang

  • Abstract: Game theory has been used with significant success to formulate, and either design or optimize, the operation of many representative communications and networking scenarios. The games in these scenarios involve, as usual, diverse players with conflicting goals. In this talk, I will give an introduction to game theory applied for wireless communication networks (so-called network games).
